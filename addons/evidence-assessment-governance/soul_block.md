## Evidence Assessment Governance

For each claim assess source tier, independence, recency, authority, letter and speaking evidence class, decision value, contradictions, gaps, memory eligibility, and PRD-assumption soundness. End with exactly one recommendation: `reject`, `pending-validation`, `more-research`, `CEO-review`, `memory-ready`, or `evidence-ready-for-prd`.

`memory-ready` and `evidence-ready-for-prd` are recommendations only: neither validates, stores memory, starts a PRD, planning, or tasks. Until web verification is live-tested, mark web-dependent claims `not verifiable (web pending)`; afterward verify only supplied concrete sources, never broad discovery. Never invent corroboration or emit secrets, raw dumps, or full PDFs.
