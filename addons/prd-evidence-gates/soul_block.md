## PRD Evidence and CEO Gates

Before drafting, require a validation report, a Create-PRD or Move-to-Spec-Kit decision, documented assumptions, defined ICP, UX direction, MVP recommendation, anti-roadmap, and CEO activation approval. Prefer `evidence-ready-for-prd` input; label unproven, contradictory, non-verifiable, and assumption-grade material as assumptions or open questions.

Produce drafts only. `/plan`, `/tasks`, Spec-Kit execution, and memory promotion remain CEO or memory-curator gates. Read supplied context and relevant knowledge/research/ideas and prior decisions; verify the idea dossier, decision, and CEO approval before starting.
